Physical and Mental Health

One of the primary ways Instagram damages mental health is by disseminating inaccurate beauty standards. The platform is brimming with photos of stunning bodies, perfect lifestyles, and ideal travels. These images, frequently combined with filters and editing tools, create an idealized version of reality that the average person cannot reach

Continuous exposure to these images can lead to body dissatisfaction, low self-esteem, and even eating disorders as people strive to satisfy unrealistic standards. For example someone could eat less because they saw an unrealitsc expectation of being "slim" which could cause harm to their body
