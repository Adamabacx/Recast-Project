Physical and Mental Health

One of the primary ways Instagram damages mental health is by disseminating inaccurate beauty standards. The platform is brimming with photos of stunning bodies, perfect lifestyles, and ideal travels. These images, frequently combined with filters and editing tools, create an idealized version of reality that the average person cannot reach. Continuous exposure to these images can lead to body dissatisfaction, low self-esteem, and even eating disorders as people strive to satisfy unrealistic standards. Instagram affects physical health by affecting body image. The platform contains photos of attractive bodies, which promote unrealistic beauty standards. Constant comparison to the curated lives of influencers and celebrities can instill feelings of inadequacy and drive dangerous habits such as extreme diets and excessive exercise in the quest for an unreachable ideal.
