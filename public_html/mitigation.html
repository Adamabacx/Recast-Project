Mitigation Techniques

While Instagram can have a negative impact, there are certain steps you can take to mitigate these effects and have a more positive relationship with it:

  • Personalize your feed to include content that inspires and informs you.
  • Limit your screen time to reduce impulsive scrolling, which helps prevent interference with your sleep or overall well-being.
  • Prioritize your mental health and seek help when needed, such as therapy or support groups.
  • Focus on face-to-face interactions with friends and family instead of relying solely on social media for connection.
  • Regularly take breaks from Instagram and all other social media platforms to engage in fulfilling activities like hobbies, exercise, or spending time outdoors.
