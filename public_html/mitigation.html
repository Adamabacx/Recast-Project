Mitigation techniques

While Instagram can have a negative impact, there are certain steps you can take to mitigate these effects and have a more positive relationship with it. One option is to personalize your feed with content that inspires and informs you. Another way to reduce Instagram's impact is to limit your screen time and avoid impulsive scrolling so it doesn't interfere with your sleep or overall well-being. Another step you may take is to prioritize your mental health and seek help when you need it, such as treatment or support groups. You can also focus on in-person connections. Instead of relying solely on social media for connection, prioritize face-to-face interactions with friends and family. Regularly taking breaks from Instagram and all other social media platforms. Use this time to do things that make you happy and fulfilled, such as hobbies, exercise, or spending time outdoors.
